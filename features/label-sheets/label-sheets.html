Print

When printing, choose Actual size / 100% (never “fit to page”). A browser cannot force this — you must pick it in the print dialog. Load the correct Celcast sheet in the printer.

Alignment

Real printers drift a few mm even at 100%. Print the test on plain paper, lay it over a blank Celcast sheet against the light: the outlines should sit inside the die-cut squares. If the whole grid is shifted, nudge below and reprint — it’s saved per sheet.

Positive X moves labels right, positive Y moves them down. If the top label prints too high, increase Y.
